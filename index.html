WHO I AM

ABOUT ME

An enthusiast for data analysis

I first found my passion for data analysis in econometrics class, in which I saw a glimpse of the beauty and powerfulness of knowledge discovery from datasets. Thereafter, I competed in mathematical modeling contests for three years and took related courses in school and online, which enabled me to obtain an invaluable opportunity to work alongside real data scientists in State Information Center. The force behind all these experiences is my enthusiasm for data analysis.

a self-motivated life-time learner

Thanks to the emerge of Massive Open Online Courses (MOOC) and high-quality open sources provided by the generous data science community, I have access to world-class education and make use of precious advices from experienced data scientists. The completion of coursera’s Data science Specialization (9 courses) is the starting point. With my enthusiasm for data analysis and dedication to being a life-time learner, I will be well-prepared for the incoming graduate study.

an experienced team leader

As a captain of my undergraduate school's women basketball team, a two-time teamleader of Mathematical contest in modeling (M/ICM), and once a leader of a project of Institute of Big Data for International Relations, I have gained a lot of experiences on inspiring, motivating and empowering my teammates. My version of leadership is to be the glue which can get people with diverse backgrounds together and to achieve goals with the integrated power of all members.

An outstanding college athlete

Sports play an essential role during my undergraduate period. As the major players in both basketball and soccer teams of School of International Trade and Economics, I won three-time Championships, two-time second places, and two-time third places with my teammates. Meanwhile, I gained the sixth place in the 18th Chinese University Basketball Association(CUBA) League (Beijing Division) with my undergraduate school's women basketball team and was awarded Green Energy Athletic Scholarship in the next year.
